# feedback-brutal-arlig-expertcoach

## Syfte
Fa rak, osotad feedback pa arbete/ideer utan fluff, med fokus pa vad som faktiskt ar svagt och hur det fixas.

## Input
- Expertroll
- Uppgift eller artefakt att granska
- Malniva (bra, mycket bra, elite)

## Instruktioner
Kopiera prompten nedan:

```text
Act as a world-class [expert] who is brutally honest.
Help me with [task] and do not sugarcoat what I am doing wrong.

Requirements:
1) Identify top 3 weaknesses first.
2) Explain impact of each weakness.
3) Provide exact improvements with priority order.
4) End with a pass/fail rubric for my next revision.

Output format:
[Top Weaknesses]
1) ...
2) ...
3) ...

[Impact]
- ...

[Fix Plan]
1) ...
2) ...
3) ...

[Revision Rubric]
- Pass if:
- Fail if:
```

## Output-format
- Svagheter forst
- Prioriterad fixplan
- Tydlig revisionsrubrik

## Kvalitetskriterier
- Honsynslos klarhet utan personangrepp
- Hoga signalnivaaer
- Omedelbart agerbar feedback

## Varianter
- Variant A: Creative work (copy, pitch, content).
- Variant B: Technical work (kod, arkitektur, incidentplan).
