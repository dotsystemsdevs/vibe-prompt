# workflow-lessonsmd-pruning-och-sammanfattning

## Syfte
Forhindra att `lessons.md` blir ett junk drawer-dokument genom regelbunden pruning och kondensering.

## Input
- Aktuell lessons.md
- Senaste sprint/period
- Hogst prioriterade riskomraden

## Instruktioner
Kopiera prompten nedan:

```text
Audit and prune lessons.md for long-term usefulness.

Tasks:
1) Group lessons by theme.
2) Remove duplicates and stale items.
3) Merge repetitive notes into one reusable rule.
4) Mark lessons as:
   - Active
   - Watchlist
   - Archived
5) Produce a short "Top 10 Active Lessons" section.

Constraints:
- Preserve high-value incidents.
- Keep total active lessons compact enough for regular context use.

Output format:
[Pruned Lessons Index]
- Theme:
  - Active:
  - Watchlist:
  - Archived:

[Top 10 Active Lessons]
1) ...
2) ...

[Removed or Merged]
- What changed and why
```

## Output-format
- Rensad lesson-struktur
- Top 10 aktiv lista
- Andringslogg for pruning

## Kvalitetskriterier
- Haller kontexten lean
- Behaller hogsignal-larande
- Minskar minnesbloat over tid

## Varianter
- Variant A: Veckovis pruning.
- Variant B: Sprintslut med strikt arkivering.
