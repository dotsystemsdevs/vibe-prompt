# larande-framstegsmatare-veckoanalys

## Syfte
Mata faktisk larandeprogress vecka for vecka sa man undviker att bara samla prompts utan att utvecklas.

## Input
- Veckans aktiviteter (vad som faktiskt gjorts)
- Tidslogg
- Gjorda ovningar/projekt
- Blockers och missforstand

## Instruktioner
Kopiera prompten nedan:

```text
You are a coding learning progress evaluator.
Analyze my past week and tell me whether I am actually improving.

Inputs:
- Study log:
- Practice done:
- Bugs solved:
- Concepts learned:
- Time spent:
- Where I got stuck:

Tasks:
1) Score progress (0-100) across:
   - Consistency
   - Concept mastery
   - Practical output
   - Debugging ability
2) Identify if I am learning or just consuming content.
3) Recommend a focused plan for next 7 days.
4) Suggest 1 metric to track daily and 1 weekly checkpoint.

Output format:
<WeeklyScorecard>
- Consistency:
- Mastery:
- Practical output:
- Debugging:
- Overall:
</WeeklyScorecard>

<RealityCheck>
- What is working
- What is fake progress
</RealityCheck>

<Next7DaysPlan>
Day 1 ...
Day 7 ...
</Next7DaysPlan>

<Tracking>
- Daily metric:
- Weekly checkpoint:
</Tracking>
```

## Output-format
- Scorecard for vecka
- Reality check (riktig vs falsk progress)
- 7-dagars justerad plan

## Kvalitetskriterier
- Arlig bedomning
- Datadrivna forslag
- Praktiskt nasta steg
- Enkelt att folja upp

## Varianter
- Variant A: Nyborjare (fokus vana + konsistens).
- Variant B: Job-focused (fokus portfolio, interview readiness, shipping rate).
