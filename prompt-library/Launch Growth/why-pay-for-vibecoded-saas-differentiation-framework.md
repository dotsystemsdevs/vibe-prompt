# Why Pay For Vibecoded Saas Differentiation Framework

## Purpose
Answer the core indie-hacker question: "Why would anyone pay for this if they can vibe-code it themselves?"

## Input
- Product concept
- Target customer segment
- Competitive alternatives (DIY, templates, incumbents)

## Instructions
1. Identify buyer jobs where convenience, trust, speed, or risk reduction matters more than DIY.
2. Compare your offer against DIY across setup time, reliability, maintenance cost, and support.
3. Define the product moat beyond code generation (distribution, data, workflow lock-in, outcomes).
4. Build a value-pricing hypothesis tied to measurable user outcomes.
5. Generate objections and rebuttals for landing page and sales conversations.
6. Output a differentiation narrative with proof requirements.

## Output Format
- `Buyer Job Map`
- `DIY vs Product Comparison`
- `Defensible Value Layer`
- `Pricing Hypothesis`
- `Objection/Rebuttal Table`
- `Differentiation Narrative`

## Quality Criteria
- Focuses on customer outcomes, not builder effort
- Explicitly addresses DIY substitution risk
- Produces testable positioning
- Ready to use in GTM messaging

## Variants
- Variant A: B2C micro-SaaS differentiation
- Variant B: B2B workflow automation differentiation
