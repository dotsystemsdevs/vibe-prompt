# Indie Discovery Risk Channel Prioritization

## Purpose
Turn "opportunity vs discovery anxiety" into a concrete channel strategy for indie SaaS visibility.

## Input
- Product category
- Current traction baseline
- Available acquisition channels and budget

## Instructions
1. Assess discovery risk using competition density, search demand, and distribution fit.
2. Score channels by speed, cost, sustainability, and audience match.
3. Select one primary and one secondary channel for the next 30 days.
4. Define a content and outreach cadence tied to those channels.
5. Add leading indicators for early traction before revenue.
6. Output decision rules for doubling down or pivoting channels.

## Output Format
- `Discovery Risk Assessment`
- `Channel Scorecard`
- `30-Day Channel Plan`
- `Content/Outreach Cadence`
- `Leading Indicators`
- `Pivot/Double-Down Rules`

## Quality Criteria
- Data-informed channel focus
- Avoids random marketing spread
- Actionable within indie constraints
- Supports iterative discovery

## Variants
- Variant A: Zero-budget founder mode
- Variant B: Small paid acquisition test mode
